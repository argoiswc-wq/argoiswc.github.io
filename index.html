ARGO

Agentic Retrieval and Graph Orchestration for Document Knowledge Systems

ARGO is a hands-on ISWC tutorial on building agentic Graph-RAG workflows for document knowledge systems, combining semantic extraction, knowledge graphs, retrieval, provenance, and MCP-based tool orchestration.

About the Tutorial

This half-day tutorial introduces practical methods for building modular, semantically grounded, and reproducible document knowledge systems.

Important Dates

Tutorial days: October 25–26, 2026

Presenters
